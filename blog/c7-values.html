The value that I want to talk about is Freedom. In my ten years of education in China, the only definition that I know about freedom is that if you are not a prisoner, you have freedom. However, I was blinded by the firewall for more than ten years. From family to school to society, as a Chinese citizen, we have very limited access to freedom. Because of Confucius, we have to "respect" the elderly, even though they have wrong values, we should not correct them, and we have to obey what they say. This is what the Chinese believe "respect". In school, you should not correct your teachers, because they should be respect. After twelf years education under this circumstances, the Chinese are educated with less creativity and barely understand what think out of the box means. Thus, I believe "freedom" is one of the most important values in one's life for his or her success.
