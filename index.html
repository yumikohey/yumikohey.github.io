ABOUT ME

I started an ecommerce business in late 2007, which led me fall in love with web development. Back then, I developed Wordpress and BigCommerce Templates just for fun.

During college, in order to develop business, I self-taught Internet Marketing. Two years ago, I started trading options, and I applied what I have learned about Internet Marketing. I shared trading diaries and technical analysis strategies on Chinese Sina Weibo. I not only attracted a lot of followers, but also got offer from Sina Weibo to become its Finance Columist. However, I turned it down, because I want to develop my career in web development industry in the United States.

Technology has grown so fast. Wordpress is a fantastic platform yet I believe that using Ruby on Rails or Meteor.js to build a web application is much more efficient than building a Wordpress theme. Thus, as an Amazon Business Specialist managed $4 Million monthly transactions, I decided to leave my job for a programing bootcamp to continue developing my interests.

So, here I am.
